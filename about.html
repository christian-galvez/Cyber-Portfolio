About Me

I’m an aspiring cybersecurity professional with a background in instructional design and IT support. I have hands-on experience in building homelabs, deploying honeypots, configuring VPNs, and analyzing network traffic with Wireshark. My goal is to bring both technical expertise and strong communication skills to roles that require security awareness and practical implementation.

In addition to technical skills, I work as an Instructional Designer, creating training for enterprise ERP systems, helping users understand day to day business processes with security best practices in mind.

I am passionate about identifying vulnerabilities, securing systems, and sharing knowledge. I continuously expand my expertise in ethical hacking, compliance, and cloud security.
